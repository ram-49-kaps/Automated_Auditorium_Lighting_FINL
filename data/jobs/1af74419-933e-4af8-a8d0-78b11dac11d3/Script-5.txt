FADE IN:
EXT. ANCIENT SKYBRIDGE – ABOVE THE CLOUDS – DAWN

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:00:00]

A colossal STONE BRIDGE stretches between two floating mountain ranges, suspended miles above rolling clouds. Wind HOWLS. Sunlight breaks through mist, painting the bridge in gold.

A weathered expedition airship hovers nearby.

INT. AIRSHIP – CONTINUOUS

[00:01:30]

Meet the team:

ARJUN (32) – Calm, sharp, seasoned explorer

MIRA (29) – Historian, emotionally driven

ROOK (35) – Muscular climber, dry humor

TAVI (19) – Young drone pilot, curious and reckless

They gear up.

MIRA
(awed)
The Skybridge of Aeralis… People thought it was a myth.

ARJUN
Every myth starts as a warning.

EXT. SKYBRIDGE ENTRANCE – MOMENTS LATER

[00:03:00]

Ancient runes glow faintly beneath their boots as they step onto the bridge.

The bridge CREAKS — not weak, but alive.

TAVI
Tell me it’s not going to collapse.

ROOK
If it does, you won’t hear me scream. Wind’ll steal it first.

They move forward.

CUT TO:
EXT. SKYBRIDGE – MIDSPAN

[00:06:00]

The bridge widens into a platform. Massive stone statues of forgotten guardians line the sides.

Suddenly—
A LOW RUMBLE.

The statues’ eyes glow.

MIRA
(reading runes)
“It judges those who cross.”

Stone arms SHIFT slightly.

ARJUN
Then walk with purpose.

They proceed carefully. The glow fades.

EXT. BROKEN SECTION – CONTINUOUS

[00:09:00]

A large portion of the bridge has collapsed, leaving a terrifying gap.

Clouds swirl violently below.

ROOK
(grinning nervously)
I live for moments like this.

He anchors a rope. One by one, they cross.

Midway—

The rope SLIPS.

Tavi dangles, panicking.

TAVI
I’m slipping! I’m slipping!

Arjun grabs him — muscles straining.

ARJUN
Look at me. Not the clouds.

They pull Tavi up.

Silence.

Then relieved laughter.

CUT TO:
EXT. SKYBRIDGE – STORM APPROACHING

[00:13:00]

Dark clouds roll in fast. Wind intensifies.

Lightning flickers inside the clouds — silent but threatening.

MIRA
Storms weren’t in the forecast.

ARJUN
This place makes its own rules.

They push forward.

EXT. ANCIENT OBSERVATORY – ON THE BRIDGE

[00:16:00]

A circular stone structure sits at the center of the bridge.

Inside: murals showing travelers crossing — some reaching the end, some falling.

MIRA
(quietly)
The bridge doesn’t kill people… fear does.

A sudden GUST blows debris inside.

The exit door SLAMS shut.

INT. OBSERVATORY – CONTINUOUS

[00:18:00]

The floor lights up. Symbols rotate.

A MECHANISM activates.

TAVI
It’s… a test?

ARJUN
A choice.

Three paths illuminate on the floor.

MIRA
Only one is safe.

They debate. Tension rises.

Finally—

ARJUN
We go together. Same path.

They step forward.

The other paths COLLAPSE.

The door opens.

CUT TO:
EXT. FINAL STRETCH OF SKYBRIDGE – SUN BREAKING THROUGH

[00:22:00]

The storm clears suddenly.

Ahead: the LAST PILLAR, glowing with ancient energy.

Golden light floods the bridge.

ROOK
Worth it.

As they approach, the bridge begins to DISINTEGRATE behind them.

They RUN.

Stone CRUMBLES.

Leaps. Slides. Near-falls.

EXT. LAST PILLAR – CONTINUOUS

[00:26:00]

They reach safety just as the bridge collapses completely.

Silence.

The clouds below settle.

The pillar HUMS softly.

MIRA
It’s not treasure…

The pillar projects visions — knowledge, maps, forgotten routes across the world.

MIRA (CONT’D)
It’s a guide.

EXT. SKY – AIRSHIP APPROACHING

[00:30:00]

Their airship returns through the clouds.

Golden light reflects off their faces.

TAVI
So… where to next?

Arjun smiles.

ARJUN
Somewhere impossible.

FINAL SHOT:
EXT. SKY – WIDE

[00:33:00]

The floating mountains drift peacefully.

Where the bridge once stood — only clouds.

The legend lives on.

FADE OUT.
END.
