FADE IN:

EXT. DERELICT SPACE STATION - ORBITING A GAS GIANT - DAY (SPACE TIME)

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:00:00]
The station, "Nexus-7," floats like a forgotten relic amid swirling purple storms of the planet below. Scratched hull, flickering lights. A small shuttle docks clumsily.

INT. NEXUS-7 - DOCKING BAY - CONTINUOUS

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:01:00]
CAPTAIN ELARA (35, sharp-eyed, no-nonsense explorer) steps out with her team: DR. KAI (42, brilliant but arrogant scientist), TECHIE LENA (28, optimistic gadget whiz), and ROOKIE MARCUS (22, wide-eyed newbie).

ELARA
(adjusting suit comms)
Alright, team. Scans show life support's holding—barely. We're here for the artifact. In, out, no heroics.

KAI
(scoffing, checking his scanner)
Heroics? This station was experimenting with quantum entanglement. If the artifact's real, it could rewrite physics. We're talking doorways to alternate realities.

LENA
(excited, fiddling with a drone)
Cool! Like, parallel universes where I'm a rock star?

MARCUS
(nervous)
Or where we're all dead. Guys, the logs said the crew vanished. Poof.

ELARA
Focus. Kai, lead on the artifact chamber. Lena, get systems online. Marcus, stick with me—watch for anomalies.

They move through dim corridors, lights humming erratically.

CUT TO:

INT. CORRIDOR ALPHA - MOMENTS LATER

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:03:30]
Echoes of distant machinery. The team passes holographic logs flickering on walls—ghostly crew members mid-sentence.

HOLO-CREW MEMBER (projection)
...the entanglement field is stable. But the echoes... they're not ours...

KAI
(stopping, intrigued)
Echoes? That's new. The artifact must be creating temporal feedback loops.

LENA
(activating a panel)
Power's at 20%. I can reroute from auxiliary—wait, there's a signal. Incoming?

A low HUM builds. The walls shimmer like water.

MARCUS
Uh, Captain? The floor's... vibrating.

Suddenly, a RIPPLE in reality—a shadowy figure appears at the corridor's end, mirroring Elara's stance.

ELARA
What the hell?

The figure vanishes. Alarms blare softly.

KAI
That's it! Quantum echo. The artifact's bleeding alternate versions of us into this reality.

LENA
So, like doppelgangers? Awesome... or terrifying?

ELARA
Terrifying wins. Move faster.

CUT TO:

INT. ARTIFACT CHAMBER - LATER

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:07:00]
A vast room with a glowing crystalline ARTIFACT at the center, pulsing blue energy. Consoles spark. The team enters cautiously.

KAI
(awestruck, approaching)
Beautiful. It's a nexus point—connecting infinite timelines.

He touches a console. The artifact HUMS louder. Holographic displays activate, showing fractured images: alternate teams succeeding, failing, dying.

LENA
(hacking in)
Logs confirm: Crew activated it, then... merged with echoes. Became something else.

MARCUS
Merged? Like, body horror?

ELARA
Kai, don't touch it yet. We extract data first.

KAI
(ignoring, scanning)
Too late. Look—these readings... we could control it. Jump timelines. Fix mistakes.

ELARA
That's not our mission. Corporate wants the artifact contained, not played with.

KAI
Corporate? They're idiots. Imagine: A world without the war. Or one where my family didn't die in the colony collapse.

LENA
(sympathetic)
Kai... that's heavy. But messing with timelines? Butterfly effect city.

MARCUS
Yeah, like, what if we erase ourselves?

The artifact pulses brighter. More echoes appear—translucent versions of the team, arguing in parallel.

ECHO-ELARA (faint, overlapping)
...don't touch it...

ECHO-KAI
...we must...

A surge— the room shakes. Marcus trips, his hand brushes the artifact.

MARCUS
Oops—

Reality GLITCHES. The team freezes, then duplicates briefly.

ELARA
Marcus! What did you do?

MARCUS
Nothing! It just... pulled me.

KAI
(excited)
It's responding. We're linked now.

CUT TO:

INT. ARTIFACT CHAMBER - ESCALATING CHAOS

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:12:00]
The glitches worsen. Echoes solidify, interacting.

ECHO-LENA (to real Lena)
In my timeline, you invented this. But it consumed you.

LENA
(stunned)
Me? No way. I fix things, not break universes.

KAI
(to Elara)
Captain, think bigger. We could merge timelines—create a perfect one.

ELARA
No. That's playing god. Lena, shut it down.

LENA
(working frantically)
Trying! But it's rewriting code on its own. AI-level intelligence?

MARCUS
Guys, look—more echoes. And they're... us, but wrong.

Echoes multiply: One team fights aliens (wait, aliens?), another dances absurdly, a third melts into goo.

ECHO-MARCUS (melting one)
Help... it's infinite...

The real team backs away.

ELARA
This is spiraling. Evac protocol—back to the shuttle!

KAI
(rebellious)
Wait! I can control it. Just one command...

He inputs something. The artifact ROARS. A portal opens—swirling void.

From it, bizarre entities emerge: Tentacled shadows, clockwork beings, cartoonish figures.

LENA
What the—? That's not sci-fi; that's nightmare fuel!

MARCUS
(panicking)
Aliens? Robots? Why is there a dancing pineapple echo?!

ELARA
Kai, you idiot! You've cracked the multiverse!

KAI
(realizing horror)
I... thought I could select timelines. But it's all of them. Every possibility.

The room fills with chaos: Echoes clash—laser fights, philosophical debates, slapstick comedy.

ECHO-ELARA (to real Elara)
In my world, you're a pirate queen. Arrr!

Real Elara shoots a confused look.

CUT TO:

INT. CORRIDOR ALPHA - FLEEING

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:18:00]
The team runs, dodging merging realities: Walls turn to jelly, gravity flips briefly.

LENA
(panting)
The shuttle's this way! But the station's destabilizing.

MARCUS
And the echoes are following!

Behind: A horde of alternate selves—heroic, villainous, ridiculous—chase them.

ECHO-KAI (villainous)
Join us! Become infinite!

KAI
That's me? I look evil.

ELARA
Keep moving! Lena, can you seal doors?

LENA
(using gadget)
On it—plasma lock!

Doors slam, but echoes phase through.

MARCUS
They ghost-mode now?!

A super-crazy echo appears: A giant, mecha-version of the team fused into one robot.

MECHA-ECHO
(booming)
We are Legion! Surrender to the void!

It fires confetti lasers—harmless but disorienting.

ELARA
This is insane! Shoot it!

They fire stun guns—confetti explodes everywhere.

CUT TO:

INT. DOCKING BAY - CLIMAX

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:22:00]
They reach the shuttle. The artifact's influence spreads: The gas giant below warps into a smiley face.

KAI
I can fix this! Reverse the entanglement!

He hacks a console remotely.

ELARA
No more experiments!

But too late—the void portal expands, sucking in echoes.

LENA
It's collapsing! We have seconds!

MARCUS
Goodbye, crazy mes!

They board. The shuttle detaches as the station implodes.

But twist: As they escape, the void follows—reality fractures ship-wide.

SUPER CRAZY ENDING:

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:24:00]
Inside the shuttle: Everything merges. Elara becomes a pirate, Kai a dancing pineapple, Lena a robot, Marcus a tentacled alien—all swapping bodies mid-sentence.

PIRATE-ELARA (with eyepatch, arrr-ing)
Yarrr, set course for the quantum horizon!

PINEAPPLE-KAI (bouncing)
But I'm fruity! This is bananas!

ROBOT-LENA (beeping)
Error: Logic overload. Dance protocol activated!

They all break into absurd dance as tentacles flail.

ALIEN-MARCUS
(tentacles waving)
In my timeline, this is normal Tuesday!

The shuttle spins into the void—screen fills with rainbow glitches, cartoon explosions, philosophical quotes scrolling like credits.

VOICEOVER (overlapping echoes)
Infinity... is... party time!

The ship vanishes into a black hole that burps confetti.

FADE TO BLACK WITH A COMIC "BOING" SOUND.
