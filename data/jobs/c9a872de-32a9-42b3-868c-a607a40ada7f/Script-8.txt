EXTREME COMEDY SCRIPT
Title: “EVERYTHING THAT CAN GO WRONG, DID”

Genre: Extreme Comedy / Slapstick / Absurd
Estimated Duration: ~45–50 minutes

FADE IN:
EXT. SMALL TOWN BUS STAND – MORNING

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:00:00]

A peaceful bus stand. Birds chirp. Calm sunlight.

A giant banner reads:
“WELCOME TO SILENT VALLEY – MOST PEACEFUL TOWN”

Immediately — a LOUD HONK.

EXT. BUS STAND – CONTINUOUS

[00:01:00]

A bus screeches to a stop.

Out jump:

RAJU (28) – Overconfident, underqualified

BUNTY (30) – Loud, zero awareness

CHINTU (26) – Genius idiot (smart ideas, wrong execution)

PAPPU (35) – Always hungry, always confused

RAJU
Alright boys. Two days. One job. Easy money.

A cow walks by and immediately STEALS Pappu’s sandwich.

PAPPU
(emotional)
It was my breakfast AND my lunch.

CUT TO:
EXT. HOTEL “ROYAL PARADISE” – MORNING

[00:04:00]

The hotel looks… suspiciously broken.

Missing letters:
“ROYAL PAR_DISE”

The RECEPTIONIST sleeps standing up.

BUNTY
Five-star, right?

Ceiling fan FALLS.

They all dodge.

RECEPTIONIST
Four-and-a-half. Fan under repair.

CUT TO:
INT. HOTEL ROOM – MOMENTS LATER

[00:07:00]

One tiny room. Four beds stacked vertically.

CHINTU
This is called vertical optimization.

Bunty jumps on a bed.
The entire stack COLLAPSES.

Beat.

RAJU
Okay. Team meeting.

They sit on the floor.

CUT TO:
INT. ROOM – PLANNING THE “SIMPLE JOB”

[00:09:30]

Raju draws a plan on a whiteboard.

RAJU
Step one: disguise.
Step two: delivery.
Step three: payment.

PAPPU
Where is food in the plan?

RAJU
After.

PAPPU
Then I quit.

CUT TO:
EXT. TOWN MARKET – AFTERNOON

[00:12:30]

They appear in RIDICULOUS DISGUISES:

Raju = fake moustache upside-down

Bunty = wedding suit + sunglasses

Chintu = lab coat + swimming goggles

Pappu = chef hat, no shirt

People stare.

BUNTY
Whose wedding is this?

RAJU
Ours. With crime.

CUT TO:
EXT. MARKET – CHAOS BEGINS

[00:15:00]

Chintu presses a button.

Instead of smoke bomb —
COLORED CONFETTI EXPLODES EVERYWHERE

Kids cheer.

Music starts from nowhere.

RAJU
WHY IS THERE MUSIC?!

A wedding procession joins accidentally.

Bunty starts dancing.

BUNTY
If life gives music — dance!

CUT TO:
EXT. POLICE CHECKPOINT – CONTINUOUS

[00:18:00]

Police stop them.

POLICE OFFICER
What’s in the bag?

Pappu opens it.

Inside:
— fake wigs
— rubber chicken
— disco ball
— 47 bananas

POLICE OFFICER
…why?

PAPPU
Emergency potassium.

Officer stares.

Lets them go.

CUT TO:
INT. BANK (WRONG BUILDING) – AFTERNOON

[00:21:00]

They storm inside dramatically.

RAJU
Everybody freeze!

People clap.

BANK MANAGER
Finally! The entertainment arrived!

Turns out:
They entered a talent show audition.

Bunty is pushed on stage.

Music BLASTS.

CUT TO:
INT. AUDITION HALL – MUSICAL CHAOS

[00:23:30]

Bunty dances aggressively.

Chintu beatboxes accidentally.

Raju trips, slides across stage.

Pappu eats snacks from judges’ table.

JUDGE
This is terrible.

AUDIENCE
(standing ovation)

Confetti cannons fire again.

CUT TO:
EXT. STREET – RUNNING AWAY

[00:27:00]

They escape.

Suddenly — REAL CRIMINALS run past.

Police chase BOTH groups.

RAJU
Why are we always included?!

CHINTU
Statistical bad luck.

They hide inside a bakery.

CUT TO:
INT. BAKERY – SLOW MOTION

[00:29:30]

Pappu sees cakes.

Time slows.

Angelic music.

PAPPU
I have arrived.

He knocks over a shelf.

ALL CAKES FALL like dominoes.

CUT TO:
EXT. BAKERY – CONTINUOUS

[00:32:00]

Explosion of flour.

Everyone white.

They look at each other.

Bunty sneezes —
LOUD FIRE ALARM

Sprinklers ON.

CUT TO:
EXT. TOWN SQUARE – EVENING

[00:35:00]

Completely soaked.

Town gathers.

Mayor approaches.

MAYOR
You idiots ruined everything.

Pause.

MAYOR (CONT’D)
But tourism hasn’t been this good in years.

Crowd cheers.

CUT TO:
EXT. STAGE – NIGHT

[00:38:00]

Banner:
“SILENT VALLEY COMEDY FESTIVAL”

The four are on stage.

Spotlights flash wildly.

RAJU
We failed the job.

BUNTY
But succeeded in nonsense.

CHINTU
Accidental success is still success.

PAPPU
Where is food?

Crowd laughs uncontrollably.

FINAL SHOT:
EXT. TOWN – FIREWORKS

[00:45:00]

Fireworks explode randomly — wrong colors, wrong timing.

The banner falls again.

The cow from earlier eats it.

VOICEOVER (ALL TOGETHER)
We planned nothing…
And that worked perfectly.

FADE TO BLACK.
END.
