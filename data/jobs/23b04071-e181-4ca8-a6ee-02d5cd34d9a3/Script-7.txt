BETRAYAL LOVE SCRIPT
Title: “THE WAY YOU LIED SOFTLY”

Genre: Romance / Betrayal / Psychological Drama
Estimated Duration: ~48 minutes

FADE IN:
EXT. CITY SKYLINE – NIGHT

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:00:00]

Rain drapes the city in shadow. Neon signs blur. Streetlights flicker like tired witnesses.

A lone apartment window glows faintly.

INT. APARTMENT – NIGHT

[00:02:00]

KABIR (31, investigative journalist) types furiously. Coffee cold. Eyes restless.

ANAYA (28, elegant, unreadable) watches him from the doorway.

ANAYA
You’re home early.

KABIR
Didn’t want the night to end without you.

She smiles — practiced, perfect.

CUT TO:
INT. BEDROOM – LATER

[00:05:00]

Low lamp light. Warm but fragile.

They lie together.

KABIR
Do you ever think about how we started?

ANAYA
Too much.

KABIR
I fell because you looked honest.

A pause.

ANAYA
And now?

KABIR
Now I trust you.

Her eyes flicker — just for a second.

CUT TO:
EXT. CAFÉ – EVENING

[00:08:30]

Dim amber lights. Rain streaks the windows.

Anaya sits across from VIKRAM (35, corporate strategist, confident).

Their hands touch.

VIKRAM
You said he wouldn’t find out.

ANAYA
(lowered voice)
He won’t. He believes in people.

Vikram smirks.

CUT TO:
INT. APARTMENT – NIGHT

[00:12:00]

Kabir returns home. The apartment is quiet.

He notices Anaya’s phone vibrating.

A message flashes:

“Did you tell him?”

Kabir freezes.

INT. APARTMENT – CONTINUOUS

[00:14:30]

Anaya enters.

ANAYA
You look pale.

KABIR
Who’s Vikram?

Silence swells.

ANAYA
A colleague.

KABIR
Why does your colleague text like a lover?

She exhales slowly.

ANAYA
Because… sometimes I get tired of waiting.

CUT TO:
EXT. ROOFTOP – NIGHT

[00:18:00]

Wind howls. City lights pulse below.

KABIR
I gave you everything honest in me.

ANAYA
And you buried yourself in work.

KABIR
So you replaced me?

ANAYA
I chose certainty.

Dark clouds roll in.

CUT TO:
INT. NEWSROOM – NIGHT

[00:21:30]

Kabir stares at a confidential file.

Company logo: VIKRAM’S FIRM.

The investigation he’s been chasing for months — corporate corruption.

Names. Payments.

Anaya’s name appears.

Kabir’s breath catches.

CUT TO:
INT. APARTMENT – STORMY NIGHT

[00:25:30]

Lights flicker. Thunder outside.

Anaya enters to find Kabir waiting.

Files spread across the table.

KABIR
You weren’t just cheating.

She doesn’t deny it.

ANAYA
I was protecting myself.

KABIR
By destroying the truth?

ANAYA
Truth doesn’t pay rent. Love doesn’t buy safety.

CUT TO:
INT. APARTMENT – ESCALATION

[00:29:00]

Voices rise.

KABIR
You used me.

ANAYA
You needed to believe in something pure. I needed power.

Silence.

The lights go out.

Only streetlight shadows remain.

CUT TO:
EXT. STREET – NIGHT

[00:33:00]

Rain pours harder.

Anaya stands under a streetlamp, soaked.

Kabir watches from across the road.

ANAYA
(shouting)
If you publish, I lose everything!

KABIR
And if I don’t, I lose myself.

Thunder cracks.

CUT TO:
INT. NEWSROOM – DAWN

[00:36:30]

Kabir’s finger hovers over “PUBLISH”.

Flashbacks:

Her smile

Their laughter

Her lie

He clicks.

CUT TO:
EXT. APARTMENT – MORNING

[00:39:30]

Police cars. Reporters.

Anaya watches from the balcony — hollow.

Kabir walks away without looking back.

CUT TO:
EXT. COURT STEPS – DAY

[00:42:30]

Anaya is led away.

She sees Kabir in the distance.

Their eyes meet.

No anger.

Only absence.

FINAL SHOT:
EXT. CITY – SUNSET

[00:46:30]

The city glows in cold blue and fading amber.

Kabir stands alone, notebook in hand.

Voiceover (soft, broken):

KABIR (V.O.)
You didn’t break my heart…
You taught me how silence lies.

FADE TO BLACK.
END.
