Main Stage Schedule - Day 1

09:00 AM - Registration and Walk-in Ambient Music
Attendees arrive and find their seats. Soft music playing over the speakers.

09:30 AM - Opening Keynote Speaker
CEO Sarah Jenkins takes the stage to present the company's vision for 2027. She will be center stage using the main podium.

10:45 AM - Morning Coffee Break
Attendees leave the auditorium for the lobby.

11:15 AM - Panel Discussion: The Future of AI
A 4-person panel discussion with industry experts. They will be seated at a table spanning the width of the stage.

12:30 PM - Lunch Break
Auditorium closed.

01:30 PM - Afternoon Walk-in
Attendees return from lunch.

02:00 PM - Audience Q&A Session
Open microphone session where the audience asks questions to the morning panelists. The moderator walks around the stage.

03:30 PM - Tech Award Ceremony
The annual employee awards are handed out. High energy, fast-paced, celebratory atmosphere as winners walk across the stage.

05:00 PM - Closing Remarks
Brief final words from the organizers. Walk out music begins as attendees exit.
