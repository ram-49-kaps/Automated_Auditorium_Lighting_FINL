FADE IN:
EXT. OLD RIVERSIDE PROMENADE – EVENING

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:00:00]

Golden sunlight reflects off a calm river. Street lamps flicker on one by one. Couples stroll. A violinist plays softly.

AARAV (30, introverted architect) sits alone on a bench, sketching the bridge ahead.

He pauses, staring at the river — lost in thought.

EXT. PROMENADE – CONTINUOUS

[00:02:30]

MEERA (28, expressive writer, warm smile) jogs by, stops, doubles back.

She peers at Aarav’s sketch.

MEERA
That bridge looks lonely.

Aarav looks up, surprised.

AARAV
It’s waiting for someone to cross.

Meera smiles, intrigued.

MEERA
So are you.

A beat. Aarav chuckles despite himself.

CUT TO:
EXT. PROMENADE – WALKING TOGETHER

[00:05:30]

They walk side by side.

MEERA
So, mysterious sketcher… what do you design?

AARAV
Buildings people pass through but never notice.

MEERA
That’s sad.

AARAV
Or honest.

She considers this.

MEERA
I write stories people feel but never say out loud.

They exchange a look — something clicks.

EXT. TEA STALL – NIGHT FALLING

[00:08:30]

They sit on plastic chairs, sharing tea.

Neon lights glow softly.

AARAV
Do you believe in coincidence?

MEERA
No. I believe in badly timed miracles.

AARAV
(smiles)
Then this is one?

MEERA
Maybe. Or maybe it’s just tea.

Their fingers brush accidentally.

Both notice. Neither pulls away immediately.

CUT TO:
EXT. RIVERSIDE – LATER

[00:12:30]

The river reflects moonlight.

MEERA
Tell me something real.

AARAV
I’m afraid of building a life that looks perfect but feels empty.

Meera nods.

MEERA
I’m afraid of loving someone who leaves without saying goodbye.

Silence — heavy, intimate.

AARAV
I wouldn’t do that.

MEERA
Everyone says that.

CUT TO:
MONTAGE – DAYS PASSING

[00:16:00]

Coffee dates

Laughing under streetlights

Reading her stories together

Aarav sketching Meera unknowingly

Almost holding hands… almost

Soft romantic energy builds.

INT. BOOKSTORE – AFTERNOON

[00:20:00]

Meera finds a sketchbook.

Inside — drawings of her.

MEERA
You draw me like I’m a place you want to return to.

Aarav looks embarrassed.

AARAV
You feel… permanent.

She smiles, then grows quiet.

CUT TO:
EXT. PROMENADE – SUNSET

[00:23:30]

Meera stops walking.

MEERA
I got a call today.

Aarav senses the shift.

AARAV
That pause before a sentence… it scares me.

MEERA
I got a publishing offer. Abroad.

Silence.

The sky turns orange-pink.

EXT. PROMENADE – CONTINUOUS

[00:26:00]

AARAV
You should go.

MEERA
That was too easy.

AARAV
Because loving someone doesn’t mean trapping them.

MEERA
And what about you?

AARAV
I’ll stay. That’s what I do.

She looks hurt.

CUT TO:
EXT. RIVERSIDE – NIGHT

[00:29:30]

They stand apart now.

MEERA
Say something selfish. Please.

AARAV
(voice breaking)
Stay.

She closes her eyes.

MEERA
That’s all I wanted to hear.

CUT TO:
EXT. TRAIN STATION – RAINING

[00:32:30]

Rain pours. Neon reflections shimmer.

Meera stands with a suitcase.

Aarav arrives, soaked.

AARAV
I redesigned my life today.

She laughs through tears.

MEERA
You’re late.

AARAV
I know. But I’m here.

EXT. TRAIN PLATFORM – CONTINUOUS

[00:35:30]

Train arrives, loud and overwhelming.

MEERA
I don’t want promises.

AARAV
Then don’t take one.

He hands her the sketchbook.

AARAV (CONT’D)
Just take proof.

She hugs him tightly.

Time feels suspended.

CUT TO:
EXT. PROMENADE – MONTHS LATER – MORNING

[00:39:00]

Same bench.

A new bridge stands in the distance — modern, elegant.

Aarav sketches again.

A shadow falls across the page.

MEERA (O.S.)
You made the bridge less lonely.

He looks up.

She’s there.

EXT. PROMENADE – CONTINUOUS

[00:41:30]

AARAV
You came back.

MEERA
I never really left.

They walk together.

Hands finally intertwine — naturally, effortlessly.

FINAL SHOT:
EXT. RIVER – GOLDEN MORNING LIGHT

[00:44:30]

The river flows calmly.

The bridge stands complete.

Two silhouettes cross it together.

FADE OUT.
END.
