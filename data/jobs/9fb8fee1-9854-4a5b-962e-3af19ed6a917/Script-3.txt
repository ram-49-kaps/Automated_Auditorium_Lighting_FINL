FADE IN:

INT. SMALL APARTMENT KITCHEN - DAY

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:00:00]
The kitchen is tiny, cluttered with half-prepped food, pots bubbling, and a giant "CATERING GIG TODAY" sticky note on the fridge. JAKE (28, enthusiastic but perpetually overwhelmed amateur chef) wears a stained apron that says "Kiss the Cook... or Run." His best friend and reluctant business partner, MIA (27, sarcastic, always one eye-roll away from quitting), chops vegetables like she's angry at them.

JAKE
(excited, waving a ladle)
This is it, Mia! Our first real catering job. Fifty people at the tech startup launch party. We're gonna crush it. Gourmet sliders, mini quiches, that fancy charcuterie thing you saw on TikTok—

MIA
(deadpan)
The one where everything looks perfect and then someone drops it on the floor? Yeah, we're nailing that vibe already.

A smoke alarm suddenly BLARES. Jake rushes to the oven, yanks out a tray of blackened mini quiches.

JAKE
No, no, no! They're... extra crispy! Trendy!

MIA
They're charcoal briquettes with cheese. We're doomed.

Jake's phone BUZZES. He answers on speaker.

JAKE
Hey, Mr. Patel! Yes, we're on schedule. Everything's under control—

MR. PATEL (O.S., frantic)
Jake! Change of plans! The venue flooded. We're moving it here—to my apartment! Right now! And... we adopted a cat last week. Super friendly. Loves people. Bring extra food!

Click. Silence.

MIA
A cat. In a tiny apartment. With fifty tech bros. And our food.

JAKE
It's fine. Cats love sliders. We'll improvise.

CUT TO:

INT. MR. PATEL'S APARTMENT - LIVING ROOM - LATER THAT AFTERNOON

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:03:30]
Chaos has begun. Folding tables crammed everywhere. Guests mill about in hoodies, sipping from red cups. A fluffy orange CAT (named "Sir Whiskers") prowls like he owns the place.

Jake and Mia set up frantically. Jake balances a tray of sliders while dodging elbows.

GUEST #1 (excited tech bro)
Yo, these sliders look fire! What's the special sauce?

JAKE
Uh... love? And aioli. Mostly aioli.

Sir Whiskers leaps onto the table, knocks over a bowl of dip. It splatters across three guests.

GUEST #2
My new Yeezys!

MIA
(under her breath)
Great. Now we're catering a crime scene.

Jake tries to shoo the cat. Sir Whiskers hisses, swats the tray—sliders fly like frisbees. One lands in a guest's drink. Another sticks to the ceiling fan, which starts spinning slowly, flinging sauce.

GUESTS
(laughing, some horrified)
What the—?!

CUT TO:

INT. KITCHENETTE - CONTINUOUS

[Approx. Timestamp: 00:06:00]
Mia and Jake retreat to regroup. Mr. Patel bursts in, holding the cat like a football.

MR. PATEL
He's just excited! New people! New smells!

The cat wriggles free, dashes out.

JAKE
We need a plan. Mia, distract the guests with... charm?

MIA
Charm? I have resting murder face. You distract. I'll salvage what food we have left.

Jake nods, grabs a microphone (it's a karaoke setup for the party).

JAKE
(into mic, too loud)
Hey everyone! Welcome! I'm Jake, your chef tonight. Quick story: I once burned water. True story. But tonight? Tonight we're elevating!

Guests chuckle. Sir Whiskers reappears, leaps onto Jake's shoulder like a pirate parrot.

JAKE
(trying to play it cool)
And this is Sir Whiskers, our... official taste-tester!

The cat bats at the mic cord. It tangles around Jake's legs. He stumbles, knocks over a stack of chairs. Domino effect—chairs topple, a table flips, charcuterie rains down like confetti.

Guests scream-laugh. Phones come out—video time.

MIA
(running in)
Jake!

She grabs a broom, tries to herd the cat. Instead, she sweeps Jake's feet out. He falls backward into a pile of cushions, cat landing on his chest purring.

JAKE
(from the floor, defeated but grinning)
Appetizers are served... floor-style.

The room erupts in applause and laughter. Someone starts a slow clap. Others join.

GUEST #3
This is the best launch party ever! Way better than free pizza!

MR. PATEL
(tearful, happy)
You guys... you turned disaster into legend!

Mia helps Jake up. The cat rubs against her leg.

MIA
(softening, smirking)
Okay, fine. We're not fired. Yet.

JAKE
Next gig: Cat-proof everything.

They high-five. Sir Whiskers meows triumphantly.

Guests swarm the spilled food—picking it up, eating anyway, toasting "to chaos!"

FADE OUT on the room full of laughter, sauce everywhere, and one very smug cat.
