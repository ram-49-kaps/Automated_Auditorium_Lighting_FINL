[ACT 1]
[SCENE 1]
(Alan enters the stage from stage left, looking absolutely furious.)
ALAN: I am so angry right now! I hate everything about this! This rage inside me is completely uncontrollable! I am furious!
EVE: Calm down, there is no need to be this incredibly angry! I hate arguments!
ALAN: No, my anger will destroy us both! I hate you! 

[SCENE 2]
(The lights flicker as a terrifying noise is heard. Eve cowers downstage, shaking.)
EVE: Oh my god! What was that? I am so scared! This is terrifying!
ALAN: Help! The fear is overwhelming. I am frightened for my life!
EVE: Run! Run away! We are in terrible danger! I am scared! 

[SCENE 3]
(They sit down together, looking incredibly depressed.)
ALAN: I am so sad...
EVE: Me too. Let us cry together in sadness.
ALAN: Indeed, so much sorrow.
